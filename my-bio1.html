A Solution Oriented, Dynamic and, Mult-tasking Engineer


Michael J. Shundi is a registered professional Engineer with over 10 years working experience in areas of technical,management & administration and, academics. He received his Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics &Telecommunications Engineering in 2009 from Dar es Salaam Institute of Technology, Tanzania. In 2019, he received his Masters' Degree in Information and Communication Engineering at the University of Science and Technology Beijing, China. His research interests include; Artificial Intelligence(AI), Internet of Things (IoT), D2D Communications, Spectrum sharing, Radar systems, 5G Networks, Blockchain Technologies and, Computer Systems Engineering.


Apart from his carrier, his passion and target has been unto young generation (Youths), Shapping and inducing positive and genuine mind-set which will help them face challenges in this modern world. He strongly believe that, youths are the ones who hold strength, therefore its a logistic reason that, imposing them with good attitudes and positive mind-set will more likely help in shapping this modern world we are now in (directly or indirectly), and hence forth make it a better place of living. Also, its significant to note that, youths are the main work force, so leading them to apply their strength positively will eventually bring positive results to the society, rather than if they apply on the negatives.
